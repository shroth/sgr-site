About Me


My true passion rests in crafting intuitive, aesthetically pleasing, and effective user experiences.

My bioengineering and healthcare consultant background have definitely influenced my approach as a UX designer - I break down problems into their component parts and make sure my team hits each step of the design process from user research to prototyping to presenting.

I am enthusiastic about taking complex problems and simplifying them for the user.
